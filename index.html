About Us

Mr Dj Shan Ug is a dynamic and versatile entertainer known for delivering electrifying performances that keep crowds energized from start to finish. With experience performing at nightclubs, weddings, corporate functions, and private events, he has built a reputation for creating unforgettable musical experiences tailored to every audience. Beyond the stage, he also brings his sound to the airwaves on X FM Uganda, part of the New Vision Group, where he connects with listeners through exciting mixes and curated music selections. Passionate about music and crowd connection, Mr Dj Shan Ug blends genres and rhythms seamlessly to keep the vibe alive wherever he performs — truly proving that he is more than just a DJ.

Driven by a deep passion for music and entertainment, Mr Dj Shan Ug blends genres seamlessly from Dancehall & Reggae to Afrobeats & Amapiano, Hip Hop, and global hits—creating a sound that keeps every crowd engaged. His ability to read the room and deliver the right vibe at the right moment has made him a trusted choice for clubs, weddings, and major events. With every performance, he lives up to his brand promise: More Than Just a DJ, delivering not just music, but an unforgettable experience.
